About Me!

My name is Ian Maigler. I am an aspiring Full-stack Developer born and raised in Victoria BC. I graduated from Camosun College with an Information and Computer Systems Technology Diploma.

My journey into programming was sparked by a deep-rooted love for problem-solving and technology. In elementary school I would acquire old broken computers to fix with the sole purpose to run minecraft servers for me and my friends to play on.
In middle school I taught myself how to solve a rubik's cube, and now every time I see a new weird version of one I have to get it just to see how long it takes me to solve.

In my free time you can find me playing games with friends, at the gym, exploring different parts of Vancouver island, or using the oxford comma.
