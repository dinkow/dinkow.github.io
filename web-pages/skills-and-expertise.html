Responsive Design

Responsive design refers to the approach of designing and developing websites or applications that dynamically adjust their layout and content based on the user's device, screen size, orientation, and platform.

Future Proofing

Responsive design helps future-proof websites and applications by ensuring they can adapt to new devices and screen sizes as technology evolves.

Accessibility

Responsive design promotes accessibility by making content available to users on various devices, including those with disabilities who may use assistive technologies or alternative browsing methods.

Media Queries

Media queries are CSS rules that allow designers to apply different styles based on the characteristics of the device, such as screen width, resolution, and orientation.
